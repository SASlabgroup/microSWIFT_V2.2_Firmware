Controller

  	FPU_EXCEPTION_OCCURRED   = bit position 25,
  	NED_WAVES_RAN_OUT_OF_MEM = bit position 26
  	
[x] Added 1 minute warmup timer to GNSS to ensure velocity data is well resolved by the time it is
    saved internally.
